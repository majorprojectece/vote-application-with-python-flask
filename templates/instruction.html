E-Voting System Instruction

Registration Guide

  1. User should register through click here.
  2. Please enter all your credentials properly because we will review every registration.
  3. After filling the form, you will be redirected to upload your identity. In this case, you need to take a selfie.
  4. We will validate this image for future verification during voting time.
  5. Then, you will be redirected to the "Thank You for Registering" page.
  6. Note: Remember your Roll Number, which plays a vital role as a Voter ID number.

Voting Guide

  1. To vote, please click here.
  2. As mentioned earlier, you need to enter your Roll Number in the form and click on submit to vote.
  3. If you are not registered, you will receive an error message indicating that you are not registered.
  4. Our system ensures that no user can vote more than once.
  5. After entering your Roll Number, you will be redirected to a page where you can choose only one candidate to vote for.
  6. Click on the "Vote Now" button and you will be redirected to a verification page with your face.
  7. Until your face matches, it will stay on that page. If your face does not match, it may indicate a problem with your identity or registration.
  8. If your face is matched, you will be redirected to a "Thank you for voting" page.
